Helia

A project started to simply get better in programming

Around January 2020 (Not the best year for humanity tbh) i decided to make a english fork of one private russian discord bot and add my own things to it , slowly it started drifting away from that base and getting its own things added on , but overall main purpose of it is getting more practice with programming overall and making a bot that just works

Few Words About Helia

Initially this bot was named OpenBot(Not a creative name i know), had the basics like moderation working and really was not much more than a english fork of a private bot , but now it is pretty functional and good working moderation and music bot
